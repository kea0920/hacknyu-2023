Take Action!

WATER

  • • Turn off faucets when not using them

  • • Take shorter showers

  • • Regularly check for leaks in pipes and faucets

ENERGY AT HOME

  • • Unplug appliances when not using them

  • • Change light bulbs to LEDs

  • • Use natural light and switch off lightbulbs when not using them

AGRICULTURE

  • • Buy groceries from local farms over large supermarkets

  • • Eat fewer animal products

  • • Buy organic foods that use less pesticides

TRANSPORTATION

  • • Try to minimize the amount of times you travel by car or plane

  • • Utilize public transportation

  • • Travel by walking or biking (also healthy excersise!)
